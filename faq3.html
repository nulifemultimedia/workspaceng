GENERAL QUESTIONS

Is account registration required?
Account registration at Workspace.ng is required for vendors and customers. This ensures a valid communication channel for all parties involved in any transactions.

Can I advertise my own workspace?
Only vendors that have registered on our platform can access this priviledge. Kindly register as a vendor on our Signup page if you want to partner with us.

What is the mode of payment for all transactions?
All prices and mode of payment for different workspace categories can be found on our Get A Space page.

VENDORS

Who can advertise on your platform?
Any registed real-estate agent or space owner, who has a workspace, which is genuine and conducive, can post it on Workspace.ng.

I want to register as a vendor - what are the steps?
The steps involved in this process are really simple. All you need to do is:
  • Register an account
  • Email us the details of your workspace
  • The next step is the approval step, which usually takes about 72 hours, before it can be uploaded on our platform.

Why advertise here?
There are a number of reasons why you should join us:
  • A great 70% flat rate for your workspaces.
  • Fast response/approval times. Many sites take weeks to process your submission. And if it gets rejected, there is another iteration. We have eliminated this, and made the process very fast. It only takes up to 72 hours for a workspace to get reviewed.
  • We are an exclusive marketplace. This means that you have a great opportunity to connect with young entrepreneurs and start-ups on Workspace.ng, which can be difficult on other platforms, and thus increase your earning potential.

CUSTOMERS

I need a workspace - what are the steps?
Getting a space on Workspace.ng is really simple. Each advertised workspace is tailored to your budget specification contains details such as location, videos and images. Once you have selected a workspace, which is to your liking, you can quickly and securely pay via our payment options.
Once the transaction is complete, you gain full access to the workspace.

Can I book for a year?
Depending on your preferences, you can book for a workspace for one month, three months and one year etc. For conference halls, bookings are also available for one day up to a week.
